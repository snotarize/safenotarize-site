What you need

Before your online notary session

  • Your completed document, preferably as a PDF.
  • A valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID.
  • A phone, tablet, or computer with camera and microphone.
  • A stable internet connection.
  • A separate email address for each additional signer, if there are multiple signers.
Important note

Sign only when the notary tells you to

Some documents must be signed during the live notary session. If your document is already signed, it may still be accepted depending on the notarial wording and platform requirements.

SafeNotarize provides online notary services, but we do not prepare legal documents or provide legal advice.

Online Notary FAQ

Answers to common questions before booking your online notary session.

What is online notarization?

Online notarization allows eligible documents to be notarized remotely through a secure live video session with identity verification.

How do I start?

Start the booking flow, enter signer details, pick a time, then upload your document. Every signer receives their own SafeNotarize invite email.

What documents can be notarized online?

Common documents include affidavits, sworn statements, Power of Attorney, travel consent forms, vehicle release forms, business documents, and other eligible documents.

What ID do I need?

You need a valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID. Examples may include a driver license, state ID, or passport, depending on verification requirements.

Can international signers use SafeNotarize?

International signers may be able to complete online notarization if they meet identity verification and platform requirements.
