Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to upload my document somewhere?

Start the booking flow, then upload your document securely. PDF is preferred. If your provider gave you a specific form, use that exact document.

Can you notarize documents for immigration packages?

We can notarize affidavits, consent forms, authorizations, POA, travel forms, and other supporting documents. We do not prepare documents or provide legal advice.

I am outside the U.S. Can I still book?

Yes. Many international signers can be verified using a valid passport, subject to platform verification requirements.

Do you support Creole and French?

Yes. We support English, Haitian Creole, and French for the online notary process.
