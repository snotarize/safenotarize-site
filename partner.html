Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to upload my document somewhere?

You can bring your document to the appointment. If your provider gave you a form, use that exact form.

Can you notarize documents for immigration packages?

We can notarize affidavits, consent forms, authorizations, and other supporting documents. We do not provide legal advice.

I am outside the U.S. Can I still book?

Yes. Many international signers can be verified using a valid passport, subject to platform verification requirements.

Do you support Creole and French?

Yes. We support English, Haitian Creole, and French.
