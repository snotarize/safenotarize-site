Frequently Asked Questions

What is online notarization?

Online notarization allows you to get documents notarized remotely through a secure video call with a certified notary public.

Is it legally accepted in all states?

Yes, remote notarization is legally recognized in most U.S. states. Check your state’s specific regulations for confirmation.

What documents can be notarized online?

You can notarize a wide range of documents including affidavits, power of attorney, real estate forms, and more.

How do I verify my identity?

You will use government-issued photo ID along with secure identity verification technology.

Is the session recorded?

Yes, sessions are recorded and stored securely as required by law to ensure authenticity and prevent fraud.
