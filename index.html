Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions before booking your online notary session.

What is online notarization?

Online notarization allows you to get documents notarized remotely through a secure video call with a certified notary public.

Is it legally accepted?

Remote online notarization is widely used and legally recognized in many cases. Acceptance can depend on the receiving agency or institution, so check your document requirements if needed.

What documents can be notarized online?

You can notarize many documents including affidavits, power of attorney, travel consent forms, business documents, vehicle release forms, and more.

How do I verify my identity?

You will use a valid government-issued photo ID along with secure identity verification technology through the notarization platform.

Can international signers use SafeNotarize?

Yes. International signers may be able to complete an online notarization if they meet identity verification requirements and have acceptable identification.

Is the session recorded?

Yes. Sessions are recorded and stored where required as part of the remote notarization process to help protect against fraud and support compliance.
