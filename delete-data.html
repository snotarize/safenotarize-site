Data Deletion Instructions

SafeNotarize | User Data Deletion Request

Effective date: July 4, 2026

You may request deletion of eligible personal information that SafeNotarize has collected through our website, booking pages, WhatsApp intake, email, phone, text message, or customer support channels.

1. How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your personal information, please email us at:

info@safenotarize.com

Use the subject line:

Data Deletion Request

2. Information to Include

To help us locate your information, include the following details in your request:

  • Your full name.
  • The email address used with SafeNotarize.
  • The phone number used with SafeNotarize or WhatsApp.
  • The approximate date of your inquiry, booking, or notarization session.
  • A short statement saying you want your eligible personal information deleted.

3. What We May Delete

Depending on your request and applicable requirements, we may delete or remove eligible information such as:

  • Contact details submitted through forms or messages.
  • WhatsApp intake information.
  • Customer support messages that are not required for business, legal, security, or compliance purposes.
  • Documents or files that are no longer needed and are eligible for deletion.

4. Information We May Need to Keep

Some information may be retained if required or permitted for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, compliance, or notarial recordkeeping purposes. For example, certain notarization records, transaction logs, payment records, audit trails, or legally required records may not be immediately deleted.

5. Verification

For security, we may need to verify your identity before deleting information. We may contact you using the email address or phone number associated with your request.

6. Processing Time

We will review your request and respond within a reasonable time. If more information is needed to locate or verify your records, we may ask you for additional details.

7. Facebook / Meta Data

If you contacted SafeNotarize through WhatsApp, Meta may also process certain information according to Meta's own policies. To manage your Meta or WhatsApp account information, please review the privacy and account settings available directly through Meta or WhatsApp.
