Haitian Online Notary FAQ

Quick answers before you start your online notary session.

Can I speak Haitian Creole during my session?

Yes. SafeNotarize can support Haitian Creole-speaking clients. The signer must still understand the document and complete any required identity verification.

Can I book or pay for someone else?

Yes. Use the booking flow for yourself, a family member, a client, or another signer. The form collects signer, payer, witness, and final document delivery information.

Can I notarize a document written in another language?

Many documents in other languages may be notarized online, but the signer must understand what they are signing and meet platform requirements.

What ID do I need?

You will need a valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID. Some international signers may use a passport, depending on platform requirements.

Can international signers use SafeNotarize?

Yes. International signers may be able to complete an online notarization if they meet identity verification requirements and have acceptable identification.

What happens after I start?

You will enter the details, enter session details, upload your document, and each signer will receive their own SafeNotarize invite email to join online.
