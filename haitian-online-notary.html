Haitian Online Notary FAQ

Quick answers before you book your session.

Can I speak Haitian Creole during my session?

Yes. SafeNotarize can support Haitian Creole-speaking clients. The signer must still understand the document and complete any required identity verification.

Can I notarize a document written in another language?

Many documents in other languages may be notarized online, but the signer must understand what they are signing and meet platform requirements.

What ID do I need?

You will need a valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID. Some international signers may use a passport, depending on platform requirements.

What happens after I book?

After booking, you will upload your document. Then SafeNotarize will send your secure session email invite before your appointment.
